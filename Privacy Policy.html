Last updated May 25, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how I (the sole developer of the StreakUp app, doing business as StreakUp OR MKAAPPS) might access, collect, store, use, and/or share ("process") your personal information when you use StreakUp, including when you:

  • Download and use my mobile application (StreakUp), or any other application of mine that links to this Privacy Notice
  • Use StreakUp. StreakUp is an app where users can save and track their own progress with the help of "Streaks". "Streaks" are activities that the user can fully customize using a name, an icon from the available selection and description. "Streaks" allow the user to track their progress by assigning which day they have completed and assigning a note for the day, so the user can describe their activity on the given day (OPTIONAL)

Reading this Privacy Notice will help you understand your privacy rights and choices. I am responsible for making decisions about how your personal information is processed. If you do not agree with my policies and practices, please do not use my Services. If you still have any questions or concerns, please contact me at streakup.app.official@gmail.com.
