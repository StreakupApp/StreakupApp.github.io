StreakUp

What is StreakUp?

StreakUp is a habit tracker built to keep you consistent, not just for a week, but for the long haul. Smooth animations, a polished interface, and smart streak logic keep you motivated and on track, making building productive habits feel effortless.

Status: Released!

After 1+ years, StreakUp is now available for Android, Go start creating new and powerful routines!

Cloud Sync

No matter the tier, we offer all of our users with Cloud Sync, so that they can access StreakUp from any other mobile device and prevent losing data.

Join StreakUp, Your Personalized Habit Tracker

It's not about being perfect, but being consistent. Create habits that fit you, note your progress, and let StreakUp remind you why you started. Watch your streaks grow and celebrate your progress every day. Start today and turn consistency into your superpower!

Create and customize your streak to your liking

Give it a name, a description, an Icon from our selection and you can start right away!

You can also set up exactly which days the streak is active on!

Make a streak with your favorite duo!

StreakUp offers a "friend streak" for premium users so that you can create and continue your habit with a friend!

Keep track of your streak with the Home Widget!

Add a widget to your phone's home screen to stay on top of your habits or activities. The widget syncs seamlessly with your app's theme and status, so it always looks just right!
